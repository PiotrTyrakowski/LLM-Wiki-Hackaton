The twist: I want to use Cognee (https://github.com/topoteretes/cognee) as a retrieval enrichment layer, not as a replacement for the markdown wiki. Karpathy's pure design relies on index.md lookup, which fails on naming mismatch, multi-hop questions, and provenance. Cognee's hybrid vector + knowledge graph fixes exactly those weaknesses.

What we are NOT doing:





Not replacing markdown with a graph DB. Markdown stays canonical.



Not exposing Cognee to the user. It's invisible infrastructure.



Not coupling the wiki's lifecycle to Cognee. The wiki is portable markdown either way.
