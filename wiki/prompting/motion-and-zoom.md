# Motion and zoom

Camera/zoom moves on the subject and on overlays. Includes "zoom punches" (sudden zoom-ins for emphasis), parallax pans, and scale animations.

_(Seed page — add recipes here as you discover phrasings that work.)_

## Starter prompt to refine

> List every motion event in this clip (zoom-in, zoom-out, pan-left, pan-right, push-in, pull-back, parallax, shake). For each, give: timestamp it starts (seconds), duration (seconds), target (subject / b-roll / overlay), magnitude (subtle / medium / heavy). Format as `t=<start> | <duration>s | <kind> | <target> | <magnitude>`. If the camera is locked off the entire clip, output `none`.
